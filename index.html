Our Services

Ambulance

Our ambulance service is available 24/7 to provide swift and reliable emergency care when you need it most. Equipped with state-of-the-art medical technology and staffed by trained professionals, our ambulances ensure safe and timely transportation to the hospital. Whether it's a medical emergency or urgent care, we are dedicated to getting you the help you need, fast.

Wards & bedrooms

Our hospital offers clean, spacious, and comfortable wards and private bedrooms designed to promote healing and relaxation. Each room is equipped with modern amenities, ensuring a peaceful environment for recovery. Our dedicated staff is always on hand to provide attentive care, ensuring every patient's comfort and well-being throughout their stay.

Heart Center

Our Heart Center is dedicated to providing comprehensive cardiovascular care. With a team of highly skilled cardiologists and state-of-the-art technology, we offer advanced treatments for heart conditions. From preventive care to complex procedures, our focus is on ensuring your heart health and overall well-being. At our Heart Center, we are committed to delivering personalized care with compassion and expertise.

IVF & Fertility

An expert team of fertility specialists, the latest technology and stringent protocols are presently yielding the highest success rates in fertility treatment in the country. Nova IVF Fertility (NIF), India's leading chain of infertility (IVF) treatment centers is known for high success rates through use of proven clinical research and ethical, transparent clinical practices.
